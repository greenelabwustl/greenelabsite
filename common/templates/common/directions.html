Metro

If you are traveling by Metro bus or train, please use the Central West End Metro Bus Center stop as your final destination. You are then able to head East to reach Taylor Street. You will head South on Taylor Street and then turn right directly onto Scott Avenue. You will see the East Building on your right.

Please see the Metro Trip Planner to find the best route for you.

Parking

Directly across the street (Scott Avenue) from the front doors of the building is a small row of parking spaces. Please park in the designated spaces that read “Reserved for Radiology Research Patients Only.” This is free to research participants and there should be an open spot for you. A research team member will place a parking pass on your windshield.
